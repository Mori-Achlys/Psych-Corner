About

This webpage is meant to give easy and useful advice on issues of mental health, general issues, or motivation and setting goals. This site will go into details of how to handle problems and difficult situations in one's life in a healthy manner. In the Mental illness help tab, it will provide more specific details on how to handle situations or emotions and impulses that arise in that field. In the General issues tab, It will provide assistance into more common problems, such as how to calm down or how to meditate and take care of yourself. Goals and Motivation provides insight on setting goals and creating motivation. If you have questions or have healthy advice you might want to share, post it in the Ask and DIscuss tab.

Disclamer

I am not a licenced professional and cannot give in-depth specific advice for serious situations. If you require immediate assistance please reach out to a health professional near you.
